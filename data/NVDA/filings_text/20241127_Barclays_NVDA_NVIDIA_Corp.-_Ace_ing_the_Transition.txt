NVIDIA Corp.
Ace-ing the Transition

The modest guide and GM commentary sets the next two quarters in a better spot as the company shifts its focus over to ramping Blackwell.

We think this was the perfect "thread the needle" guide, as the company both appeases investors on Jan and sets up April in a more reasonable spot. The company won't commit to Hopper growth in Jan but are indicating a Blackwell ramp better than the original guide of several billion. The two areas of pushback here are GMs, which start the year off in the "low 70s" before recovering later into the Blackwell ramp, and Networking, where we see a Q/Q decline likely due to some rip and replace H200 before Blackwell leads to upgrades. We think the stock works off of this as the NT is now de-risked and investor focus can now shift back to the upside potential of the greater Blackwell cycle, where we see volumes really stepping up significantly in 2H25.
